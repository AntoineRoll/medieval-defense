# Roadmap & Ideas

## Post-MVP Features (Next Steps)
- [ ] Implement Rock-Paper-Scissors damage modifiers (defined in combat.md)
- [ ] Add more enemy types:
  - Fast Enemy: 40 HP, 120 speed, 5 damage
  - Tanky Enemy: 150 HP, 40 speed, 15 damage
- [ ] Add tower types (static defensive structures with attack radius)
- [ ] Implement town progression:
  - **Stage 2: Town (Guild)**: Unlock Architects/Blacksmiths/Merchants Guild mechanics
  - **Stage 3: City (Ruler)**: Prince/Bishop/Burgher ruler choices
- [ ] Add more unit types (beyond MVP Foot Soldier/Archer/Cavalry)
- [ ] Implement buildable buildings (player-placed structures beyond Town Center)

## Progression Details to Implement
### Guild Stage (Town)
- **Architects' Guild**: Heavy siege weapons, defensive ballistics, Engineering unlocks
- **Blacksmiths' Guild**: Faster resource gen, construction speed, Industry unlocks
- **Merchants' Guild**: Expanded borders, boosted gold flow, Trade unlocks

### Ruler Stage (City)
- **Prince Ruler**: Military honor, +25% unit damage
- **Bishop Ruler**: Faith/science, +25% building HP
- **Burgher Ruler**: Economy, +25% gold gain

## Map Expansion
- **Infinite Map**: Ideally, the map should be infinite, procedurally generated as the player expands
- **Procedural Generation**: Terrain, resources, and decorative items generated dynamically
- **Exploration**: Reveal new areas as Town/City expands
- **Biomes**: Different terrain types (plains, forests, mountains) affecting gameplay

## Potential Future Ideas
- Hero units with unique abilities
- Resource gathering (wood, stone) for building
- Multiplayer co-op defense
- Weather system affecting combat (rain reduces archer accuracy)
- Wave boss enemies (high HP, special attacks)
- Tech tree for upgrading units/buildings
